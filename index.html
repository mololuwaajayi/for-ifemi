For You

🎵 Ordinary

We do not always speak the same language,

our seasons fall on different days,

our silences carry separate weather.

Yet here you are, still reaching,

not because the road is easy,

but because you believe something worth keeping

does not ask to be perfect first.

I have watched you hold the door

when I was slow to walk through.

I have seen you choose, again and again,

the quiet grace of trying.

So let this be my offering,

not a promise of sameness,

but a witness to your constancy:

I see you. I am grateful you stay.

With love and honesty,

His Name, Spoken into the Sky

Alani

Ọlúwásèyífùnmi · Eni ti a ye ko ni
🎵 Meji Meji, Brymo
Yoruba

Alani! Eni ti a ye ko ni!
Omo ti oju ipade aanu ri,
Ti aaro ko ri i, osan ko dari i pada,
Sugbon ale mu u wa, gbe e kale lara wa.

Alani! Omo ifarada!
Awon to beere re ko gbagbe,
Awon to n reti re ko yo oju kuro,
Nitori ireti ti o gbe ninu okan ko seewadii.

Alani! Ona re ko roun,
Sugbon ese re ko dawo duro.
Okan re fowon kan ohun ti o fe,
O n wa oju ona ninu okunkun,
Idi niyi ti mo fi n pe o loruko re.

Alani! Eni ife fi i san gbese,
Eni ti okan re n gbogun ti idarudapo,
Ti o n so pe: a o se re dara.
Mo ri o. Mo jerisi si e.
Ope ni pe o wa.

English

Alani! The one we survived to have!
A child that the eyes of mercy found,
Morning did not bring him, noon did not return him,
But evening carried him home, and laid him in our arms.

Alani! Child of endurance!
Those who sought him did not forget,
Those who awaited him did not look away,
For the hope they carried in their hearts was beyond measure.

Alani! Your path has not been easy,
Yet your feet have not stopped moving.
Your heart reaches for what it wants,
It finds a way through the darkness.
This is why I call you by your name.

Alani! One whom love repays its debt through,
Whose heart makes war against confusion,
Who says: we will make it better.
I see you. I bear witness to you.
I am grateful that you are.

An oriki is not just a praise. It is a map of who you are.
And Ifemi, yours was written in pure joy.
